Mods are split into different types, depending on what they rely on. ALL mods require BepInEx.

Deli

Deli mods require Deli 0.4.1, and are denoted by a .deli file extension.

Some mods may have this extension, but also require OtherLoader. These mods include gun mods and custom ammo mods.

Sideloader

Sideloader mods require Sideloader and XUnity ResourceRedirector. These mods include sound and skin mods, and very rarely gun mods, while having the file extensions of .hotmod and .h3mod.

Asset Bundles

Asset bundles are loaded through OtherLoader's legacy loading and have no file extension (MyFile.txt has the .txt extension, while MyFile does not). These bundles are almost always a firearm of some sorts, and can be paired with an accompanying .manifest file. This .manifest file is not needed for load the mod.
